Welcome to Karoc Marathon Corner Deli!

Located at 402 W. Hoewisher Rd in Sidney, Ohio, the Karoc Marathon
boasts a selection of over 400 craft beers. The store also
has a Growler Station where 16 different draught beers are served.
Try it in store, or you can take it home!





The Karoc Marathon also has over 200 different wines
(including regional wines) and over 25 types of premium cigars.





The car wash is another attractive feature. It is open 24/7
for your convenience. Get $2 off any carwash on Sundays!
